Custom Cake Consultations


Looking for an unforgettable cake to top off your next event? We are here to bring your vision to life.

What can I expect at my consultation? During cake consultations, clients collaborate one-on-one with an experienced cake consultant who will help create and design your vision into the perfect cake for any special occasion. Customers will also sample a variety of our cake samples as well. What should I bring with me? We recommend that you bring inspirational pieces to assist in making your vision a reality. Helpful items include:
  • Color Swatches
  • Event Invitation
  • List of Ideas
  • Photo Inspiration
  • Cake Topper/Cake Jewelry
 How far in advance do I need to place my cake order? For standard cakes, contact our bakery at any time. While we request that orders are placed a week in advance, please give us a call and we'll be happy to accommodate your order. If you're placing a custom cake order, we require two week's notification. For specific inquiries, please contact 0714862655. How do I order a cake? First, we recommend viewing our cake galleries for inspiration! Please make note of the selected cake design and number and call our cake consultation during our business hours (Mon-Sun from 9AM - 5PM) or can order online. If the cake you are looking to purchase requires special customization, please book a consultation. Please keep in mind that cakes that require a consultation must be ordered at least 2 weeks in advance. What is your store policy? All food sales are final. All payments are non-refundable. Payment for Custom orders must be paid 2 weeks in advance. Any cancellations must be made 2 weeks in advance.
